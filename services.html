✚ Our Process

How a STCKLY Engagement Works

01
Needs Assessment
Our clinical consultants visit your facility to assess your exact device requirements, bed strength, and clinical workflow.
02
Custom Proposal
We present a tailored device package — right specifications, right brands, right budget — within 48 hours of your site visit.
03
Supply & Installation
Certified installation by our biomedical engineers with full commissioning documentation and regulatory handover package.
04
Staff Training
Hands-on training for your clinical and maintenance teams before the device goes into patient service.
05
Ongoing AMC Support
Scheduled preventive maintenance, calibration certificates, and 24/7 breakdown support under your AMC plan.
